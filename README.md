# NimkatBoard

A free tactical football board for building formations, drawing plays, and sharing them as images.

---

## About

We're **Nimkat** — a team of Iranian and Farsi-speaking football content creators who love the game and love talking about it.

When we started breaking down tactics on camera, we kept running into the same problem: none of the tools out there felt right for what we needed. So we built our own. NimkatBoard is the result — a clean, fast tactical board made specifically to help us explain formations and plays clearly in our videos.

It's free, and we hope other football fans find it as useful as we do.

Follow our work on YouTube: [youtube.com/@NimkatYT](https://www.youtube.com/@NimkatYT)

## Installing on Windows

1. Download `NimkatBoard_x.x.x_x64-setup.exe`
2. Run the installer
3. If Windows shows a **"Windows protected your PC"** SmartScreen warning, click **More info** → **Run anyway**

This warning appears because the app is not yet code-signed. It is safe to proceed.

---

## Installing on macOS

1. Download the `.dmg` for your Mac:
   - **Intel Mac:** `NimkatBoard_x.x.x_x64.dmg`
   - **Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4):** `NimkatBoard_x.x.x_aarch64.dmg`
2. Open the `.dmg` and drag NimkatBoard into your Applications folder

**First launch — required workaround:** macOS blocks apps that are not code-signed. To open it the first time:

1. Right-click (or Control-click) the app in Applications
2. Click **Open**
3. Click **Open** again in the dialog that appears

You only need to do this once. After that, the app opens normally.

---

## Automatic Updates

The app checks for updates every time it launches. When an update is available, it downloads and installs in the background — no action required on your part. The app restarts automatically to apply the update.

---

## Closing the App

Clicking the close button hides the app to the system tray so it stays ready to open instantly.

To fully quit: right-click the tray icon → **Quit**.
